## MONTHLY MONITORING

Track these metrics for each keyword:

1. **Ranking Position** - Where you rank (1-100)
2. **Search Volume** - Monthly searches
3. **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** - % of clicks from impressions
4. **Impressions** - Times your page appeared in search
5. **Clicks** - Actual clicks to your site
6. **Conversion Rate** - % of clicks that convert
